Welcome to the OSINT CTF Challenge!

Organized by Kerala Police Cyber Security Wing Cyber Division



There are 15 challenges in total. After you complete each challenge, show the answer to the CTF organizers. Once they confirm the answer, you will receive the url for the next challenge. Each challenge is worth 10 Points. After 10 minutes you can ask for a Hint. It will reduce your points by half. The first team to complete all 15 challenges or the Team with the maximum points at the end of the competition wins the CTF.

  • Do not use any AI chatbots or tools
  • Do not use your mobile phones during the competition
